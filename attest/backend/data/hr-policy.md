# HR Policy

## Vacation Policy

Employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation accrues at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Unused vacation days can be carried over for up to 6 months into the following year.

## Sick Leave

Full-time employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not accrue and cannot be carried over. Employees must notify their supervisor within 2 hours of the start of their shift if they are unable to work due to illness.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval. Remote work requests should be submitted through the HR portal and include a brief justification. Employees working remotely must maintain regular business hours and be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM).

## Benefits

Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance, dental insurance, and vision insurance after 90 days of employment. The company contributes 80% of the premium for individual coverage and 50% for dependent coverage.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted annually in Q4. Employees receive a performance rating from 1-5, with 5 being outstanding. Ratings of 4 or above are required for promotion consideration.

## Termination

Employees may resign with 2 weeks' notice. The company may terminate employment with or without cause. Employees terminated without cause receive severance pay of 1 week per year of service, up to 4 weeks total.
