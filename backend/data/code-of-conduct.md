# Code of Conduct

## Our Values

- **Integrity**: We act with honesty and transparency
- **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity
- **Excellence**: We strive for quality in everything we do
- **Collaboration**: We work together as a team
- **Innovation**: We embrace new ideas and continuous improvement

## Expected Behavior

- Be inclusive and welcoming to all colleagues
- Listen actively and seek to understand different perspectives
- Give and receive constructive feedback respectfully
- Take responsibility for your actions and apologize when needed
- Focus on what is best for the team and company
- Show empathy and support for colleagues facing challenges

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation
- Derogatory comments or jokes based on identity
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Intimidation, bullying, or abuse of power
- Disrespectful disagreement or personal attacks
- Sharing confidential information without authorization

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. Document the incident with dates, times, and details
2. Report to HR, your manager, or any company leader you trust
3. For anonymous reporting, use the ethics hotline: 1-800-ETHICS
4. All reports will be investigated confidentially

## Investigation Process

- Reports are reviewed within 2 business days
- Investigations are conducted by HR or external investigators
- All parties are treated fairly and given opportunity to respond
- Findings are documented and appropriate action taken
- Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited

## Consequences

Violations of this code may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Mandatory training
- Performance improvement plan
- Suspension without pay
- Termination of employment
- Legal action if warranted

## Professional Conduct

- Maintain professional boundaries with colleagues
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose when they arise
- Protect company assets and confidential information
- Represent the company professionally in all interactions
- Comply with all laws and regulations

## Remote Work Considerations

- Maintain professional communication in virtual settings
- Respect colleagues' time zones and working hours
- Be mindful of background during video calls
- Keep virtual meetings inclusive and respectful
- Follow the same conduct standards as in-person

## Leadership Responsibilities

Leaders are expected to:
- Model expected behavior consistently
- Address conduct issues promptly and fairly
- Create an environment where concerns can be raised safely
- Support colleagues who report issues
- Participate in training and development on inclusive leadership

## Policy Review

This code is reviewed annually by:
- HR Committee
- Diversity and Inclusion Council
- Executive Leadership Team
- Employee feedback through surveys
