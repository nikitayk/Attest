## Prioritization Framework

Features are prioritized based on:
1. Customer demand and feedback
2. Strategic alignment with company goals
3. Technical feasibility and resource availability
4. Competitive landscape
5. Revenue potential

## Release Cadence

- Major releases: Quarterly
- Minor releases: Monthly
- Bug fixes: Weekly as needed
- Hotfixes: Immediately for critical issues

## Stakeholder Communication

- Monthly roadmap review with product team
- Quarterly roadmap updates to all stakeholders
- Customer advisory board input on priorities
- Competitive analysis and market research

## Success Metrics

- User adoption and engagement
- Feature usage statistics
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Revenue impact of new features
- Technical performance metrics
